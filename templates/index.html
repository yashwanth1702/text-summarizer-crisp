ABOUT

Crisp is a Text summarizer that generates a concise and precise summary of voluminous texts while focusing on the sections that convey useful information, and without losing the overall meaning.Text summarization aims to transform lengthy documents into shortened versions, something which could be difficult and costly to undertake if done manually.
